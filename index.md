Rimurus Menu is a popular script for 2Take1Menu written in lua <br>
Here lays all the features and releases of Rimurus Menu

## Player

Name  | Description
------------- | -------------
Visions  | A sub menu containing 700+ visions
Field Of View  | Change your cameras field of view
Player Opacity  | Change your peds opacity. 0% will be fully invisible
Reset Player Opacity  | Reset the above back to default (100%)
Become a cop  | You will report your own crimes, get a generic cop voice, get a cop-vision-cone on the radar, and you will be unable to shoot at other cops. SWAT and Army will still shoot at you. 

## Weapon

Name  | Description
------------- | -------------
Silent Aimbot Ped | very op silent aimbot for use on peds
Silent Aimbot Player | very op silent aimbot for use on players
Object Weapons  | sub menu containing object weapons that are added by the included .oiv
Cartoon Gun  | shoot cartoon like particle effects from your gun
Dildo Gun  | Shoot dildos from your gun because why not
Ped Gun  | Launch peds from your gun 
Paint Gun  | Paint the vehicle you're looking at a custom colour
Object Gun  | Shoot a random object from your gun 
Delete Gun  | Delete the entity you shoot
Grapple Gun  | Flings you to wherever your bullet impact is
Dust Gun  | Cause dust streams to appear when you shoot
